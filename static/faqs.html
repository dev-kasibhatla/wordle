FAQs

A few clear answers.

Short, plain, and useful.

What is this?

A public Wordle engine, solver, and API. You can play it, inspect it, and run your own copy.

Is the source open?

Yes. The full project lives on GitHub, and the footer links straight to it.

Can I rely on the public API?

For demos and experiments, yes. For anything critical, ship your own deployment.

Why the limits?

Speed matters. Shared systems stay fast when one client cannot take the whole room.

What should I never send?

Secrets, private data, or anything you would not post in public.
